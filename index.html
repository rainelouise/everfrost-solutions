Frequently Asked Questions

Common concerns about air conditioning systems.

How often should I service my AC unit?

▼

It is recommended to service your AC unit at least once a year to ensure optimal performance.

What are the signs that my AC needs repair?

▼

Common signs include unusual noises, weak airflow, warm air blowing, and increased energy bills.

How can I improve my AC efficiency?

▼

Regular maintenance, cleaning or replacing filters, and keeping vents unobstructed can improve efficiency.

Why is my AC leaking water?

▼

Clogged drain lines, dirty filters, or refrigerant issues can cause AC units to leak water.

What temperature should I set my AC to?

▼

For optimal comfort and energy savings, set your AC between 24-26°C (75-78°F).

Can I install an AC unit myself?

▼

While possible, professional installation ensures safety, efficiency, and warranty protection.
